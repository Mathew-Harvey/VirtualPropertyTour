About RealVision3D

Pioneering the future of real estate visualization technology

Our Story

RealVision3D was founded in 2018 by a team of passionate architects, real estate professionals, and game developers who recognized the transformative potential of Unreal Engine for property visualization.

Our journey began when co-founders Michael Chen and Sarah Martinez—an architect and game developer, respectively—collaborated on a visualization project for a luxury property. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional 360° tours and static renderings, they envisioned a more immersive and interactive way to experience properties virtually.

Drawing on their expertise in spatial design and real-time rendering technology, they assembled a team of specialists to develop a proprietary capture and modeling process that could transform real properties into photorealistic, interactive environments. After months of R&D, RealVision3D was born.

Since our launch, we've grown from a small startup to an industry leader in real estate visualization technology, serving clients across the United States. Our commitment to innovation and quality has established us as the preferred partner for real estate professionals looking to set their listings apart with cutting-edge virtual experiences.

Our Technology Stack

The innovative tools behind our virtual experiences

Capture Technology

Our property capture process combines multiple cutting-edge technologies to create comprehensive digital twins of physical spaces:

  • Custom-modified aerial drones with 8K cameras for exterior and large interior spaces
  • 360° photogrammetry cameras for interior spatial capture
  • LIDAR scanners for precise spatial measurements and depth mapping
  • HDR lighting capture for realistic lighting reproduction
  • Material scanning for accurate texture reproduction
  • High-precision measurement tools for architectural accuracy

Our proprietary data fusion process combines these inputs into a unified dataset that serves as the foundation for our 3D modeling work.

3D Modeling & Rendering

Our development team uses industry-leading software to transform raw capture data into interactive virtual environments:

  • Unreal Engine 5 for photorealistic rendering and real-time interaction
  • Automated mesh generation and optimization for different devices
  • Dynamic lighting simulation for time-of-day and seasonal variations
  • Virtual staging capabilities for empty properties
  • Material replacement tools for renovation visualization
  • VR/AR compatibility for immersive experiences

Our team includes former AAA game developers who apply their expertise in creating detailed, performant 3D environments to real estate visualization.

Web Platform & Delivery

Our custom-built web platform ensures reliable, high-performance delivery of interactive 3D content:

  • Cloud infrastructure optimized for 3D content streaming
  • Global CDN for low-latency delivery worldwide
  • Adaptive quality settings for different devices and connections
  • Analytics dashboard for tour performance tracking
  • Enterprise-grade security for client data protection
  • Easy integration APIs for real estate websites and MLS

Our platform is designed to make high-fidelity 3D content accessible to anyone with a web browser, without requiring special software or plugins.
