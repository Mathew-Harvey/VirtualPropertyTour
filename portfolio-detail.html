Project Overview

This breathtaking oceanfront villa presented both an incredible opportunity and a unique challenge for our 3D capture team. The client, a luxury real estate agent specializing in high-end properties, needed to market this exceptional $12.5 million estate to international buyers who couldn't easily visit in person.

Our team utilized our premium capture package, including drone photography, LIDAR scanning, and detailed photogrammetry to create a comprehensive digital twin of the property. The virtual tour features both interior and exterior spaces with photorealistic detail, allowing potential buyers to experience the property's sweeping ocean views, architectural details, and luxurious amenities from anywhere in the world.

Technical Highlights

  • Full-property capture including 6 bedrooms, 8 bathrooms, and expansive grounds
  • Custom lighting conditions (day, dusk, and night views)
  • Virtual staging options for key living spaces
  • Interactive information hotspots highlighting premium features
  • Aerial drone capture integrated into the 3D model
  • Oceanfront and beach access visualization

Project Timeline

How we created this virtual experience

Day 1

Initial Consultation & Planning

Meeting with the real estate agent to understand property details, target audience, and marketing goals. Developed a custom capture plan optimized for oceanfront visibility.

Day 2-3

On-Site Capture

Full-day property capture with our technical team using drone photography, LIDAR scanning, and photogrammetry. Multiple lighting conditions captured for day/dusk/night variations.

Day 4-8

3D Modeling & Development

Data processing and 3D model creation in Unreal Engine. Texture mapping, lighting simulation, and environmental details added for photorealistic quality.

Day 9

Client Review & Revisions

Initial model presented to the agent for feedback. Minor adjustments to lighting and the addition of several information hotspots requested and implemented.

Day 10

Deployment & Integration

Final virtual tour deployed to our hosting platform. Integration with the agent's website, MLS listing, and social media channels completed.

Technical Specifications

The technology behind this virtual experience

Capture Equipment

  • DJI Matrice 300 RTK drone with Zenmuse P1 camera
  • FARO Focus S 350 LIDAR scanner
  • Canon EOS R5 with 8K video capability
  • Custom lighting equipment for evening capture

3D Development

  • Unreal Engine 5 with Nanite micropolygon rendering
  • Lumen global illumination system for realistic lighting
  • Automatic LOD generation for performance optimization
  • Custom material development for accurate textures

Hosting Infrastructure

  • AWS CloudFront global CDN for low-latency delivery
  • Adaptive streaming for different network conditions
  • WebGL rendering with fallbacks for older devices
  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing

Analytics Integration

  • Detailed viewer tracking and engagement metrics
  • Heatmap analysis of popular viewing areas
  • Conversion tracking for lead generation
  • Integration with agent's CRM system
