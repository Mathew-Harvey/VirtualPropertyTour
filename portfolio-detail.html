Property Details

This breathtaking oceanfront villa offers unparalleled luxury living with stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean. Masterfully designed by renowned architect Richard Meier, this contemporary masterpiece showcases floor-to-ceiling glass walls, soaring ceilings, and an open concept floor plan that seamlessly connects indoor and outdoor living spaces.

The main level features a grand living area with a fireplace, formal dining room, gourmet chef's kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances, and a spacious primary suite with a private terrace, dual walk-in closets, and a spa-like bathroom.

The upper level houses four additional en-suite bedrooms, each with ocean views and private balconies. The lower level includes a state-of-the-art home theater, wine cellar, fitness room, and staff quarters.

The outdoor oasis boasts an infinity-edge pool, spa, summer kitchen, multiple terraces, and direct beach access. Additional features include a 4-car garage, smart home technology, hurricane-resistant windows, and a backup generator.

Location & Neighborhood

This exceptional oceanfront villa is situated in the prestigious neighborhood of Miami Beach, offering the perfect blend of privacy and convenience. The property enjoys 100 feet of pristine beachfront with direct access to the Atlantic Ocean.

The neighborhood is known for its luxury estates, world-class dining, and upscale shopping. It's just minutes away from the vibrant South Beach district, renowned art galleries, and cultural attractions.
