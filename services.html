Our Process

How we transform your property into an immersive digital experience

1

Property Capture

Our technicians visit your property with advanced LIDAR scanners and drone equipment to capture every detail with precision and accuracy.

Duration: 2-4 hours depending on property size
2

Data Processing

Our team processes the captured data, creating accurate 3D models, point clouds, and initial spatial mapping of the property.

Duration: 1-2 days
3

3D Modeling & Texturing

Our artists create photorealistic textures and models, ensuring every surface, material, and detail is accurately represented.

Duration: 1-3 days
4

Unreal Engine Implementation

The property model is imported into Unreal Engine where we add lighting, interactivity, and optimize performance across devices.

Duration: 1-2 days
5

Quality Assurance

Our QA team tests the virtual tour thoroughly, ensuring all interactive elements work correctly and the experience is seamless.

Duration: 1 day
6

Delivery & Integration

We deliver the final virtual tour and assist with integrating it into your website, MLS listings, or other marketing channels.

Duration: Same day

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our 3D virtual tour services

Our standard turnaround time is 2-3 business days from property capture to delivery of the final virtual tour. Premium clients receive priority service with 1-2 business day turnaround, and Ultra clients can expect same-day or next-day delivery. Express and rush options are available for additional fees.

For best results, the property should be clean, well-staged, and free of clutter. All lights should be working, blinds/curtains open for natural light, and personal items minimized. Our team will provide a detailed preparation checklist before the scheduled capture session.

Clients can access the virtual tour through a direct link we provide, or via an embed code that you can place on your website, social media, or MLS listing. The tours work on all modern devices including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones without requiring any special software or plugins.

Yes, we offer update services for completed tours. Minor changes like text updates or hotspot adjustments are usually included free of charge for active clients. More significant changes like virtual staging adjustments or adding new features are available for additional fees. Major changes requiring a new capture session would be quoted as a new project.

Standard 360° tours use stitched panoramic photos with limited interaction. Our Unreal Engine-powered virtual tours create true 3D models of the property that viewers can navigate freely, examine from any angle, and interact with at a much deeper level. Our technology also allows for features like virtual staging, lighting changes, measuring tools, and renovation visualization that aren't possible with simple 360° photos.
